How the AI Matching Engine Works

Our platform analyzes key terms in your resume, bio, work history, and education, comparing them directly against recruiter job requirements. It evaluates candidate fit instantly to deliver a compatibility score and matching details.

1. Skill Extraction

The system scans resume submissions to parse skills and profile keywords.

2. Profile Comparison

Algorithms evaluate the overlap of profile keywords against job prerequisites.

3. Ranked Compatibility

Results display a compatibility score (0-100%) and details explaining the fit.
